PyCon India is the primary Python conference in India. A purely volunteer effort, it is being hosted for the third time in India, and will attract some of the best Python developers in India and around the world.

The conference will take place in Symbiosis, Pune on 16th, 17th and 18th September 2011.

The conference will consist of tutorials, full length presentations, shorter lightning talks and open sprints and BoFs.
